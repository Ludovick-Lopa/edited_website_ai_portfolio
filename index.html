About the Course

The "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity" course by The GRAPH Courses is an intensive 8-week live cohort program. It's designed to equip professionals with the skills to use no-code AI tools for automating tasks, analyzing data, and enhancing communication in a research and work environment.

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations: Learn how LLMs work, generate text, create project websites, and design presentations using AI assistants.
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis: Master AI tools for analyzing data, creating automated dashboards, and generating reports and visualizations.
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis: Use AI for literature searches, text classification, and knowledge synthesis to extract insights from large text collections.
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots: Build custom chatbots and research assistants by leveraging advanced LLM architecture and knowledge bases.
  • AI for Task Automation: Design AI agents and complex workflows to automate repetitive tasks, email responses, and web-based actions.
  • GenAI for Communication and Outreach: Create AI-generated research summaries, scientific figures, and video abstracts to share your findings.
