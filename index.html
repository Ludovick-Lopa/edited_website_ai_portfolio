About the Course

The "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity" course by Ludovick Lopa is an intensive 8-week live cohort program. It's designed to equip professionals with the skills to use no-code AI tools for automating tasks, analyzing data, and enhancing communication in a research and work environment.

The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs), building websites, automating data analysis, using AI for text analysis and literature reviews, creating custom GPTs and chatbots, and designing AI-driven communication assets.
